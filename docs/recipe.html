Gram’s German Chocolate Cake

German sweet chocolate cake

Serves Cake

This recipe came from my dad's mother who got the recipe from her mother. My grandma started teaching me how to make this cake when I was 12 year old.

Equipment

  • Stand mixer with paddle attachment
  • Rubber spatula
  • Mixing bowls (various sizes)
  • Whisk
  • 3 round cake pans (lined with parchment)
  • Use a sauce pan to boil the needed amount of water
  • Oven
  • Measuring cups & spoons
  • Egg separtor to get egg yolks

Ingredients

  • Bakers German sweet chocolate
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups sifted cake flour

  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 egg whites
    njk stiffly beaten

Instructions: German sweet chocolate cake

  1. Melt chocolate in hot water slowly or eles it will burn, when cooling take pan off burner to cool nicer for hot water

  2. Cream butter & sugar till fluffy. stand alone mixer or any mixer available

    3. eek You can use any mixer that is available

  3. Add egg yolks, 1 at a time, beating well. Blend in vanilla & chocolate.

    eek Blend in cooled chocolate and vanilla (clarifying that the chocolate must be cooled )

  4. Sift flour with baking soda & salt. Add alternately with buttermilk/choco mixture. Beat till smooth. rubber spatula recommended

  5. Fold in by hand beaten egg whites.

    6. eek "Fold" means gently combine with a spatula in circular motion.

  6. Pour into 3 round cake pans lined with parchment paper. for 350°F30-35 min minute. Cool in pans 15-20 min minute. Flip and remove paper.
